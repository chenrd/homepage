### 自动编译class文件
1、IntelliJ IDEA -> Preferences... -> Build,Execution,Deployment -> Compiler -> Make project automatically(勾选)
2、搜索快捷键关键字 -> Maintenance 使用快捷键:Registry... ->  compiler.automake.allow.when.app.running(勾选)
